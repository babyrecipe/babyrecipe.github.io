How to make Vegetable Stock For Babies and Toddlers

January 1, 2015 4 Comments

When I first searched how to make vegetable stock I thought it might be a big procedure requiring lot of time. But when I actually made it, it was so easy and a good base for all soups and curries.

This vegetable stock can be used to thin out purees, can be added to all Vegetable Soups, curries, khichdi and Porridges.

This vegetable stock can be frozen and thawed when needed.

How to make Vegetable Stock for Babies and Toddlers?

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • 2 large carrots
  • 1 bunch bulb of spring onions
  • 8 cloves of garlic – minced
  • A small piece of ginger grated
  • 2 bay leaves
  • A pinch of salt
  • Oil – 1 tablespoon.

Method

1. Chop the vegetables into 1 inch cubes

2. Heat oil in a pot.

3. Add all the veggies, cook in for 5 minutes.

4. Add the water and salt.

5. Allow it to simmer for around 30 minutes in low heat.

6. Strain the vegetables by pressing down in a sieve.

7. The stock can be frozen in a ice tray, after it is fully frozen can be transferred to ziplock bag and can be used to thin out puree, add to porridges etc.
