Fruit and Nut Granola Recipe for Kids

February 25, 2015 1 Comment

The fruit and nut granola combines the goodness of dry fruits and nuts.

It is rich in Iron and other nutrients.

It is an energy bar and makes a great travel snacks for children.

How to make Fruit and Nut Granola Bar for kids?

Dry Ingredients:

  • 1½ cup Oats
  • ¾ cup Whole Wheat Flour
  • ½ tsp Baking Soda
  • ½ cup almonds, flaked
  • ¼ cup Dried dates,
  • chopped¼ cup dried Raisins

Wet Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup Sugar
  • ¼ cup Brown sugar
  • ¼ cup Olive oil
  • 4 tbsp Milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla essence

METHOD:

1. Grease and line a 7x11x2 inch cake tin with baking parchment.

2. Take all the dry ingredients in a bowl and whisk. Keep aside.

3. In a large bowl, add milk, oil, sugar and vanilla. Beat till the sugar is dissolved.

4. Fold in the dry ingredients and mix well to form a thick mixture

5. Spread it in the lined tin and level it.

6. Baked in a pre heated oven at 180 c for 40 minutes.

7. Garnish with chocolate syrup, flaked almonds or chocolate chips.

8. Store in an air tight container for upto 10 days.
