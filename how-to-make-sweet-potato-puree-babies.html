How to make Sweet Potato Puree for Babies?

February 5, 2015 3 Comments

When can I give Sweet potato to my baby?

Sweet Potatoes can be given after 6 months.

How to choose Sweet Potatoes for Baby?

Choose sweet potatoes that are firm to touch and without any black spots, decay or cracks.

Benefits of Sweet Potatoes for babies

1. They are one of the highest sources of Vitamin A and betacarotene.

2. They have high fiber content and may work to prevent constipation.

3. Sweet Potatoes tend to release the energy slowly so can be a good food for babies.
