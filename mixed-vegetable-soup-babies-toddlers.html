How to make Mixed Vegetable Soup for Babies & Toddlers?

Mixed Vegetable Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 1 carrot finely chopped
  • Â 7-8 French beans finely chopped
  • 2-3 florets of broccoli finely chopped
  • 3-4 button mushrooms finely chopped (can be avoided for babies)
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • Â 2 tsp butter
  • Salt
  • Black pepper powder/ Jeera Powder
  • 1 tbsp milk (can be added for babies older than 1 year)
  • 1/2 glass water
  • Veggies of your choice can be added.


Method

1. In a pan heat some butter and a drop of oil.
2. Add onions and saute till they become light pink in colour.


3. Add grated ginger and cook till the raw smell leaves.
4.Add all the chopped vegetables and stir fry for two minutes.


5.Add water and let it boil.


6.Add salt and black pepper powder.
7.Let the water simmer on low flame till all the veges are tender.
8.In the end, add milk and allow it to boil a little.


9.The soup is ready.
10.Serve in a soup bowl and garnish with a sprig of coriander.

For babies, when you introduce soup, sieve off the top water and feed a couple of times. Then the whole soup can be blended in a mixie or blender and can be given.
