Paneer Kheer Recipe

January 11, 2015 Leave a Comment

This Paneer Kheer Recipe is made with Cottage Cheese or Paneer, milk and dry fruits powder is a good nutritious dessert for your children.

Paneer can be given to babies above 8 months of age; to know more, check out our post on when you can introduce paneer for kids. This Paneer Kheer can be given to kids above 1 year as it uses milk in the Recipe.

Benefits of Paneer for children

  1. Paneer is the richest source of proteins, with 100 g of paneer yielding about 18 g of protein.
  2. Paneer is rich in calcium and helps in teeth and bone development.

Paneer Kheer Recipe for Kids

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup paneer ( cottage cheese), grated
  • 1 tsp Dry fruits powder
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 deseeded date.
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder

Method:

1. In a heavy bottom pan boil the milk.

2. Add finely chopped date in the milk and let it simmer for 10 minutes.

3.Â Add grated paneer and cook for 5 minutes.

4. In the end add dry fruit powder and sugar and mix well.

5. Serve chilled or warm.
