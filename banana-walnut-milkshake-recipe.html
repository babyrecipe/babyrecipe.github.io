Banana Walnut Milkshake Recipe

January 12, 2015 12 Comments

Banana Walnut Milkshake is a high energy drink which combines the goodness of banana and walnut. It is a most effective food to give during Fever.

How to make Banana Walnut Milkshake?

INGREDIENTS

  • ½ cup banana, peeled and quartered
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp walnuts, chopped
  • 4-5 ice cubes
  • Honey to drizzle

METHOD

  1. Put all the ingredients into a blender and blend for 1 minute.
  2. Pour into a glass, drizzle with honey and serve immediately.
