If you thought smoothies are only for adults, think again! Smoothies are an easy way to ensure fussy toddlers get their daily quote of fruits and veggies. Since we know how picky kids over one can be, weâ€™ve got together a bunch of fruit and vegetable smoothies that look as good as they taste!

4 Kid-Friendly Fruit and Vegetable Smoothies

Kiwi Broccoli Banana Smoothie

This smoothie is sweet and sour in taste. It has the sweetness from banana and mild sourness from the kiwi. Broccoli not only imparts a lovely green color to the smoothie but also adds in to the nutritional value. Almonds give a nutty flavor to the smoothie and oats give a smooth texture.

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 2-3 florets of steamed broccoli
  • 4-5 almonds
  • 1 Kiwi, washed, peeled and sliced
  • Â¼ cup oats
  • Water as required

Method:

In a blender add all the ingredients and pulse to get a lump free smoothie. Serve immediately.

Papaya Carrot Banana Smoothie

This one is sweet and has a vibrant orange color which appeals to kids. The smoothie is full of antioxidants and vitamins, with all the goodness of papaya, carrot, pumpkin and banana. Candied apricot is also added to the smoothie.

Ingredients :

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup chopped papaya
  • 1 small carrot ( steamed)
  • 1/4 cup chopped and steamed pumpkin
  • 1 candied apricot
  • water as required

Method:

In a blender add all the ingredients and pulse to get a lump free smoothie. Serve immediately.

Beetroot Pomegranate Banana Smoothie

This smoothie is a mildly sweet and fiber rich smoothie with a beautiful baby pink color. Cooked beetroot imparts the color and dried figs make it sweet and give a distinct taste.

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1/4 cup chopped cooked beetroot
  • 2 dried figs ( Can soak the figs in hot water)
  • 1/2 cup pomegranate seeds
  • water as per requirement

Method:

In a blender add all the ingredients and pulse to get a lump free smoothie. Serve immediately.

Cauliflower Cashew Banana Smoothie

This is a sweet and delicious smoothie which is a perfect hiding place for veggies like cauliflower for fussy eaters. Boiled potato and steamed cauliflower along with banana give a smooth creamy texture to the smoothie and impart a milky white color. Cashew nuts give a nice nutty and rich flavor to the smoothie.

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 4-5 cashewnuts
  • 2-3 florets of cauliflower, steamed
  • 1 small potato , boiled
  • 1/4 cup oats
  • water as required

Method:

In a blender add all the ingredients and pulse to get a lump free smoothie. Serve immediately.

The veggies are steamed prior to adding to the blender to make the smoothie, ensuring easier digestion for the younger tots. All the smoothies of this series use plain water as the base and are free from salt and sugar, while also being vegan. These fruit and vegetable smoothies can be given to kids above 12 months of age.
