Chilled Mango Cucumber Soup Recipe

This yummy mango cucumber soup makes an easy way to make kids eat cucumber.

Ingredients

  • 2 mangoes, peeled and pitted
  • 2 cucumbers
  • 3 tbsp finely chopped red onion
  • 3 tbsp fresh lime juice, or to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander

Method

  1. Finely chop 1 mango and 1 cucumber and set aside.
  2. Chop remaining mango and cucumber into big pieces.
  3. Blend them with 1/4 cup water in a blender until smooth.
  4. Transfer to a bowl and stir in finely chopped mango and cucumber, onion, lime juice, and 2 cups cold water.
  5. Place bowl in a larger bowl of ice and cold water and stir until cool.
  6. Just before serving, stir in coriander and 1Â¼ tsp salt.
