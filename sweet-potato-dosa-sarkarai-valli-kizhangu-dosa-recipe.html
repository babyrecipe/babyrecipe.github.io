  • Dosa Batter- 1 cup
  • Oil or Ghee 1 tsp

Method

1. Wash the sweet potatoes well
2. Peel the skin
3. Chop the sweet potatoes into small pieces


4. Grind the chopped pieces with little water and salt.
5. After the sweet potatoes have blended well add them to dosa batter and mix well.


6. Let the batter sit for few minutes.
7.Meanwhile place the dosa tawa on the stove.
8. Pour a laddle of the batter and sprinkle little ghee or Oil
9. After the one side is cooked . flip over to cook the other side.


Serve hot with chutney or sauce

Add cleaned and powdered jaggery to the batter.
