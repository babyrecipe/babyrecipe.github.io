Groundnut Wheat Porridge Powder Recipe

December 3, 2014 9 Comments

The Wheat Groundnut Powder porridge can be given from 8 months and is an ideal travel food for babies.

How to make Groundnut Wheat Porridge Powder at home?

Ingredients :

Wheat 60 g.

Green gram dal or Moong Dal 25 g.

Groundnut 15 g.

Method of preparation :

1. Roast wheat , green gram dal and groundnuts

2. Powder these roasted ingredients separately.

3. Mix thoroughly.

4. Store in dry and airtight container.

How to cook the instant porridge?

  • Take about 4 Table spoons of mix.
  • Add 100 ml of boiled hot water to get a thick consistency. Please stir continuously as you mix water with the powder to prevent lump formation
  • Add a teaspoon ghee to increase the energy content.
  • Feed the infant with the spoon.
  • The instant porridge can also be fed to the child after reconstituting with dal, water, gravy, milk etc.
  • Mashed fruit (Banana, Chiku, Papaya, Mango, etc.could also be added to the food).
