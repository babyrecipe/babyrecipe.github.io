Review – Maharaja Whiteline Smart Chef Food Processor

February 18, 2016 Leave a Comment

Gone are the days when women spent all day slaving in the kitchen. No sooner were the breakfast dishes cleared away, than it was time to start with lunch prep! It’s an understatement to say that modern kitchen appliances have made life so much easier for us! One such appliance is the food processor, which many women claim to be their best friend or even a magic genie that can do anything! In an Indian kitchen, food processors can do a wide array of functions – chopping, slicing, juicing, kneading and much more. And if you’ve been thinking about getting your very own genie, you’re in luck! Today, we have a review of the Maharaja Whiteline Smart Chef Food Processor.

About the Brand

Maharaja Whiteline is India’s second most well known brand in small appliances and has been creating well thought out home solutions since its inception in 1976. The brand has many kitchen appliances, home comfort and garment care appliances.

Our Experience with the Maharaja Whiteline Smart Chef Food Processor

The food processor comes in a predominantly red box, reflecting the processors red and white color scheme. The back of the box lists the various functions of the food processor in detail.

The food processor comes with a Demo Video that is very useful to explain how the various parts of the food processor can be used. The instructions are quick but clear and the video works better than a thick instruction manual any day!

Here are all the parts of the food processor laid out on my kitchen counter top. As you can see, there are many parts which include the main machine, jars of different sizes and an assortment of blades and slicers. An illustrated booklet explains the function of each part separately.

The main unit of the food processor has anti slip feet which is great if you have smooth counter top like mine. Another well-thought out feature of the food processor is the safety lid. It keeps your fingers safe when you’re using the large bowl of the processor.

Coming to the big processing bowl, it is a very versatile part and can perform many functions including kneading, chopping and grating. It is made of clear plastic with a handle and fits in snugly within the food processor, which is useful especially if you don’t have too much space.

Chopping vegetables is something we need to do for every meal of the day, and this is a breeze with the food processor.

Having a food processor also promotes healthy eating. Since the kneading and chopping is taken care of, you don’t need much time to whip up a simple, home cooked meal. Besides, you can take the freshest seasonal fruits and vegetables and make smoothies for your family.

The entire unit is compact and looks kind of cute! The color theme is mainly white with some red, and is likely to fit in with most kitchen color schemes. It’s advisable to store all the parts in a single box so that they’re always accessible.

Price and Availability

The Maharaja Whiteline Smart Chef food processor is available at leading kitchen appliance stores. You can also buy it from Amazon, Flipkart and Snapdeal.

Verdict

The Maharaja Whiteline Smart Chef food processor works great and the makers have thought about Indian cooking styles in detail. The food processor can save a lot of time and make cooking a pleasant experience. With the coming summer, no one wants to spend a lot of time in the heat, so this is the perfect time to get your personal genie!
