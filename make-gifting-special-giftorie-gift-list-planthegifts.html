Gifting doesn’t have to be a chore anymore; not when you have Giftorie to help you find and reserve the perfect gift!

I distinctly remember my baby shower when I was pregnant with my son, more than eight years ago. It was a pretty grand event, and although i was waddling and feeling huge, the celebration did much to lift my spirits! There was yummy food, fresh flowers, gorgeous sarees, jewelry – and of course, lots of gifts!!!

After the event, we sat down to open up the packages, only to find 5 bottle warmers, 7 sets of baby toiletries and 6 identical baby footprint kits – turned out that the nearby department store was having a sale on them! The worst part was that none of the gifts were actually useful to us. It was sad that our guests put in so much time, money and effort. All the things that I really wanted, a bottle sterilizer, a bathing chair, carry cot etc. had to be bought by myself, which was not easy to do since I had to manage a tiny newborn too!

Now if only there was a way the guests could be directed towards stuff we actually needed!!!

The Ultimate Gifting Solution – Gift Registries

Gift registries are a common phenomenon in the USA, with more than 90% weddings having a dedicated registry. Besides weddings, baby showers, honeymoons and even kids’ birthdays now use gift registries – including Indians who live abroad!

And now, finally, we have the ultimate solution to the problem of unwanted gifts and the answer to my prayers – Giftorie gift listing services – right here in India! Giftorie is a universal gift registry platform that can be used to prepare wish lists for gifts as well as to choose a gift. How does Giftorie work?

Now that we know that there is a satisfactory solution to all our gifting problems, let’s take a detailed look at how it works. Anyone who has an upcoming celebration can prepare a gift list. It can be a wedding, honeymoon, new home, new baby, birthday or a graduation. Whatever the occasion, Giftorie will help you create a gift list of everything that you need (and maybe some things that you don’t really need but would love to have!). With Giftorie, you can add products from any online store to your list – with just a click of a button! This magical button is called the ‘Add To Giftorie’ button that gets added to your bookmark bar. There is more – you can even take pictures of something you’ve seen in a store and add it to your list – how cool is that!

Once your gift list is ready, you can share it among your loved ones – through Facebook, through eCards, on your event website or through a friend. They can now simply choose what they want to gift you from the list, so you’re sure to get something you want!

If gifting for an occasion like a housewarming, you can ensure that the home owner doesn’t end up with a bunch of mismatched stuff. She can get everything to match and create a harmonious environment in her home – all thanks to intelligent gifting!
