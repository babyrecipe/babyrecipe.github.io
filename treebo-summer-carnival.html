Enjoy as a Family at the Treebo Summer Carnival!

May 30, 2016 Leave a Comment

They say that travelling expands your mind, but if you’re travelling with kids, there are many other things which get expanded – your patience, for instance. And of course, your improvisation skills, considering you are travelling with little humans who are set in their ways!

Travelling as a family has many advantages, and your kids actually learn a lot more than you think. It encourages independence, adaptability and observation, while opening their minds to new thoughts, places and people. But of course, these advantages come at a price, namely the sanity of parents! Here’s a quick look at the biggest challenges of travelling with young kids:

1. Ensuring they eat at least some healthy food and don’t end up with diarrhoea

2. Trying as far as possible to keep to their usual nap & meal times so they don’t turn into cranky monsters

3. Keeping them entertained during the up journey, the down journey, waiting times and while at the hotel

4. Ensuring that the afore-mentioned entertainment doesn’t consist solely of screens and similar devices

And nearly all these factors need to be considered during your choice of hotel, since that is going to be your ‘home’ while you’re on vacation. As you may well know, there are several hotels and hotel chains out there, but finding one that is clean, has kid-friendly dining options, and sufficient entertainment can be pretty hard!

But what if we were to tell you that you can, indeed, find a place like that? And you don’t need to move heaven and earth to find it – here it is!

Presenting the Treebo Summer Carnival – a fun-filled hotel stay offer for families with kids. This offer is available across all Treebo Hotels in India, and is perfect to make your vacation a memorable one!

The folks at Treebo have really analyzed what parents need when travelling with kids, which is why they’ve got a lot of goodies when you opt for this offer:

  • Two kids below 8 years old can stay free of charge
  • Every day, you get a yummy snack hamper that contains a juice, chocolate, chips and croissant
  • Every kid is given a special surprise toy to keep them entertained
  • You get a booklet of vouchers from various kid-centric companies with cool offers exclusively for Treebo guests
  • The kids get free art supplies which they can use to participate in their National level Art competition.

Well, what did we tell you – all your travelling challenges gone, with a tap of Treebo’s magic wand! Your kids get toys, snacks and have an opportunity to get creative with art! Parents can chill too, as all Treebo hotels have guaranteed amenities like AC rooms, TV with DTH, free breakfast and free Wi-Fi.

With so much fun happening at the Treebo Summer Carnival, you don’t need to look for other places to stay! With the kids occupied, parents can get some time to relax, so that the whole family enjoys a super, fun-filled holiday!
