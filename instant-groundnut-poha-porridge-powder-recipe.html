Instant Groundnut Poha Porridge Powder Recipe

December 5, 2014 32 Comments

This instant groundnut poha porridge powder can be given after 8 months and is an ideal travel food.

How to make Groundnut Poha Porridge Powder at Home?

Poha & Groundnut Porridge Powder

Ingredients :

Poha (Rice Flakes) 100 g.

Bengal gram 30 g.

Groundnut 20 g. (Roasted)

Method of preparation:

1. Roast Poha (roast them to a light brown but do not overburn them) and grind.

2. Grind roasted Groundnut (without skin) & Bengal gram separately.

3. Mix all thoroughly. Store in dry airtight container.

Add hot water to a tablespoon of the powder when needed, fruit purees can be added for sweetness.
