Sorghum / Jowar Porridge Recipe for Babies

December 5, 2014 7 Comments

The sorghum or jowar porridge can be given after 8 months to babies. It is also an ideal travel food for babies.

Other Names of Sorghum

1. Egyptian Millet in English.

2. Cholam in Tamil.

3. Jwari in Marathi.

4. Jola in Kannada.

5. Jonnalu in Telegu.

How to make Sorghum / Jowar Porridge at home?

Ingredients :

Roasted Jowar Flour 45 g.

Roasted Bengal gram Powder 20 g.

Roasted Groundnut 10 g.

Method of preparation:

1. Mix all the ingredients thoroughly

2. Store in air tight container.

When needed add hot water to a tablespoon of powder, mix well and serve.
