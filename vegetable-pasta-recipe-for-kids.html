Vegetable Pasta Recipe for Kids

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 1 tomato finely chopped
  • 1 carrot grated
  • 1/2 capsicum finely chopped
  • 7-8 French beans finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup fusili
  • 1 tsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp mixed herbs
  • Black pepper powder
  • 1tbsp olive oil


Method:
1. Take enough water in a pan for boiling pasta.

2. Add little oil and salt in the water.

3. Once the water boils add the pasta to it.

4. Let it simmer on low flame.

5. When the pasta is cooked strain it in a sieve and wash it under cold running water.


6. On the other side in a kadhai add olive oil and saute onion till light brown.

7. Now add tomato to it and cook till done.

8. Add beans and capsicum to it and cook.Add carrots and let it cook till tender.

9. Put in all the seasonings Salt Black pepper powder and mixed herbs.


10. Add tomato ketchup and mayonnaise.Â Cook for 2-3 minutes.

11. Add pasta and mix.


Your pasta is ready.



You can skip ketchup and mayonnaise for younger kids.
It will be a instant hit for an all kids party.
