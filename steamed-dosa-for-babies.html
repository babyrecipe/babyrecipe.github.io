Method for making Steamed Dosa

1. Sterilize all the utensils that are being used for cooking and serving using eitherÂ SterilizerÂ or boiling them in hot water.

2. Heat the Tawa.

3. After it is hot, spread out the batter just a small circle.



4. After the holes arise pour ghee around the sides and over the dosa.



5. Cover it with a lid and cook.
