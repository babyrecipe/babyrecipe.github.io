Mango Muesli Shots Recipe

June 1, 2016 Leave a Comment

The season of mangoes is still on and we must make the most of it before it’s over! This tropical fruit is loaded with health benefits due to it’s various essential nutrients like pectin, Vitamins A, C and E. It’s also a good source of dietary fiber and antioxidants. Thankfully, mangoes are yummy, which makes them a kid-friendly food! Now if you’d like to add some fun to a plain mango, read on for our mango muesli shots recipe!

Mango Muesli Shots Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 large (slightly firm) mangoes, peeled, diced & chilled
  • 1/2 cup muesli (I used Kellogg’s)
  • 2 tbsp tutti frutti bits
  • 2 tbsp chocolate chips

You will also need shot glasses to serve.

Method:

1. In a clean and dry shot glass, put in a layer of 6-7 cubes of mango, followed by 1 tsp of muesli.
2. Top with a few chocolate chips & some tutti frutti bits.
3. Repeat the layers one more time.
4. Serve chilled.
This recipe combines cereal with fruit, but in an unusual way. You can replace the muesli with wheat flakes or cornflakes, depending upon your child’s preference. Instead of adding tutti frutti bits & chocolate chips you can add some chopped nuts or more fresh fruit like kiwi, cantaloupe, strawberries, etc. Make it as colorful as possible and watch it vanish!!
