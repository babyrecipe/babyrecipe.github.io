Poha / Rice Flakes Porridge Powder Recipe

December 5, 2014 39 Comments

Poha or Riceflakes Porridge can be given from 6 months after introduction of all basic foods. It is an ideal Travel food for babies.

How to make Poha or Riceflakes Porridge powder at home?

Poha Porridge Powder

Ingredients :

Poha / Rice Flakes 100 g.

Moong dal 30 g.

Method of preparation :

1. Roast Poha and Moong dal separately.

2. Grind the ingredients individually and mix thoroughly.

3. Store in a dry airtight container.

Add hot water to a tablespoon of the powder when needed, fruit purees can be added for sweetness.Â
