Homemade Instant Rava Idli Recipe

Instant Smiley Rava Idli


Ingredients

  • 1 cup suji/rava
  • 1 cup curd
  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 1 carrot grated
  • 1/2 capsicum finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam masala
  • Ghee for greasing the Idli plate

Method


1. Mix all the ingredients except fruit salt and keep aside.


2. Heat a little water in the idli steamer and grease the molds with ghee.


3. Pour 1 tbsp of batter in each mold leaving some space for it to rise.
4. Use peas for making eyes a small triangular piece of carrot as the nose and a tomato piece for lips.


5. Now steam the idli for 10 minutes or till your toothpick comes out clean. Your smiley idlis are ready and are perfect for kids birthday parties or also as a finger food.

You can also make these without the smiley faces.
