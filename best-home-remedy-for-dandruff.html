WHAT IS DANDRUFF?

Dandruff is a common condition that causes dry white or grey flakes to appear in the scalp or hair. While it is difficult to treat it completely, it can be effectively controlled.

EFFECTS OF DANDRUFF:

  • Dandruff causes severe itching of the scalp.
  • It may spread to face, neck and upper back causing itchy pimples.

HOW TO CONTROL DANDRUFF

  • It is always better to use separate towel for head.
  • Change pillow cover atleast twice a week.
  • Clean the combs regularly.
  • Wash hair atleast thrice a week.
  • Always maintain a separate comb.
