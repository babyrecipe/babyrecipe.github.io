When Can I give Papaya to my Baby?

Papaya can be given to babies from 6 months after having introduced other fruits Apple, Pear has been given to the baby.

How to select Papaya?

Select a papaya that is yellowish green in color and allow them to ripen in counter.

As soon as they are ripe, use them as they may rot faster than expected.

Nutritional Benefits of Papaya for Babies

  • It is rich in Vitamin C and A and antioxidants.
  • They are rich in Fiber.
  • It helps in digestion and prevents constipation.
  • They are rich in minerals and potassium, magnesium and copper.

How to make Papaya Puree for Babies?

  1. A baby would require half fruit for one serving.
  2. Peel the skin and remove the seds
  3. Chop them into cubes.
  4. Either Mash them with fork or blend it to a puree.
  5. They taste great if combines with banana.
