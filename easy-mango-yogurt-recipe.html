Easy Mango Yogurt Recipe

May 24, 2015 10 Comments

This Easy Mango Yogurt Recipe is another super healthy dessert to surprise your little one this summer.

There is no added sugar or preservative.

It combines goodness of curd and yumminess of mango and can be given to babies from 8 months of age and for toddlers

Easy Mango Yogurt Recipe for Babies and Toddlers

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cup mango
  • 1 cup hung curd

Method:
1. Cut mango into cubes and freeze till frozen solid.
2. Combine curd and mango in a food processor until smooth.


3. Pour in a glass.
4. Garnish with mango and a mint sprig.
