Bottlegourd Puree or Louki Puree for Babies

Bottle gourd or Lauki, as it is more commonly known in India, is a very popular vegetable in Indian households, and with good reason! It is a powerhouse of nutrients and bottlegourd puree or lauki puree is perfect for babies as it doesnâ€™t have an intense taste or smell.

How to select Louki or Bottlegourd for babies?

Select a small lauki with skin free of blemishes and black spots.

Nutritional benefits of Louki / Bottlegourd for Babies

  • Non allergenic and perfect food
  • It contains more than 90% of water and is thus very good for digestion in babies
  • It helps relieve constipation.
  • It is also rich in minerals like calcium, magnesium and Vitamin A,C.

How to Make Lauki Puree/Bottlegourd Puree for Babies?

Method

  1. Wash and peel the Lauki
  2. Cut the peeled lauki into small cubes.
  3. In a pressure cooker, add the lauki and moong dal with 50 ml of water.
