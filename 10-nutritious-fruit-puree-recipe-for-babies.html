How to make Fruit Purees for Baby at home?

  • It is always advisable to steam the fruits for puree except for a few fruits like Banana, Avacado and Kiwi.
  • Steaming helps preserve the nutrients and fruit purees serve as a wholesome meal for the baby in the early months of weaning.
  • After steaming the fruits, they can be blended in a blender or mashed with little breast milk or formula.
  • Make sure it is runny at first and as days go by you can increase the consistency of the puree.
