Baby Product Essentials for the Indian Parent

February 10, 2016 Leave a Comment

Finally, the great day has come – you are ready to welcome a new baby into your home! You and your spouse must be feeling so happy and proud and a mixed bag of so many emotions. You’re looking forward to holding your little one in your arms and doing everything for her. Joyful as it is, caring for a young child can be exciting and exhausting together! To relieve stress and make this stage enjoyable, it makes sense to be prepared with a set of necessary articles that you will need regularly. Some products may not need frequent replacement, like clothing, but things like hygiene and skin care products need to be bought often.

Here is a list of items that you can stock up on to avoid last minute rushes to stores. You can also shop online for these; many online pharmacies allow you to not just buy medicines online, but other personal care and baby care products as well.

Baby Product Essentials for the Indian Parent

The Essentials: Diapers and Wipes

Babies are soft and cute and cuddly, but they can also be very messy! Babies need frequent diaper changes, and you also need to stock up on soft wipes to use during each diaper change. Buy large packs of diapers of the appropriate size, along with wipes. Keep tabs on the stock you have left and shop for new packs before you run out. A changing table or mat gives you a clean surface to change baby, and is also easy to clean. Diaper rash creams also help to deal with angry diaper rashes.

For The Bath: Shampoos and Body Wash

A no-tears shampoos and gentle body wash that cleans your baby’s hair and skin without causing irritation are must haves. You can buy these individually or get a baby bathing set that contains everything within it.

Skin and Hair Care: Oils, Lotions, And Creams

Nothing is as soft and silky as a baby’s skin, and you will need to make sure that their skin stays that way. Use oils, lotions and creams meant for babies; they help maintain the oil and moisture balance in your child’s skin. If you need a skin care product that will last long, use oils. They take a longer time to be fully absorbed into the skin. Creams and lotions are a mix of oils and other liquids, with a higher oil content in creams than in lotions. Lotions are more readily absorbed into the skin, while creams stay longer. Use baby hair oil in small quantities for soft scalp.

Feeding The Baby

Buy feeding bottles and sterilizers, along with different sizes of nipples and soothers. Once your baby starts weaning, buy packs of baby formula and baby food. Try to get organic baby food that is free of chemicals and preservatives to ensure that your child gets a healthy and nutritious diet. While you’re at it, get some bibs and burp cloths as well.

Baby Care Accessories

Your baby may soon start teething and will need teethers and pacifiers to chew on. When his teeth start appearing, you will need to begin a dental hygiene routine. Buy soft bristled toothbrushes and baby toothpaste to gently clean the child’s teeth when you bathe him. You can also shop for combo sets of soft brushes and combs to groom your baby’s hair.

Baby Medical Care

You take great care of your baby to ensure that she stays healthy and strong, but babies can still get sick, even the healthiest ones. Be prepared for such emergencies with a a baby thermometer to monitor her temperature. if she is sick. You can buy medicines online for your little one as well, but be sure you have your pediatrician’s go ahead for medicines like safe-to-use paracetamol drops.

Gifts for Baby

Are you planning on visiting your best friend’s new baby? Buy gift packs of bath products or skin care products that’ll actually be useful! These will usually consist of shampoos and body wash along with lotions and creams. It’s a great gift not just for the baby but for the parents too, who need to keep replenishing their stock of baby stuff!

The new addition to your family is a special little person. They take up a lot of your time and energy, but it’s also a job that is eventually satisfying and rewarding. You are caring for a new life, a person who will always be a special part of your life forever. By being well prepared with the essentials mentioned above, you can rest easy that you have everything your baby needs and you can focus on enjoying being the parent of your little angel.
