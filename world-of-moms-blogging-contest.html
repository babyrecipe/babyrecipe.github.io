World of Moms Blogging Contest

November 4, 2015 Leave a Comment

“There are two ways of spreading light: to be the candle or the mirror that reflects it.” – Edith Wharton

This quote by Edith Wharton makes a lot of sense, doesn’t it? Even if you can’t be the light, you can still reflect someone else’s! It was this quote that I remembered when I read about the World of Moms blogging contest, called ‘This Little Light of Mine’. After reading the instructions, I knew I had to participate, and I did!

If you’re new to the World of Moms, here’s a little introduction about the website. World of Moms (also referred to as WOM) is a specially tailored community of and for Indian Moms, with a ton of information about, what else – being a Mom! There are recipes, articles on health and development, tips to have fun and answers to a lot of Mommy questions. There’s also a lot of humor and fun stuff for Moms, so it’s a great place to hang out when you’re feeling low! There’s even a Classifieds section where you’ll find information about the latest facilities and stores in your city. Besides all these, there are regular contests held here, with lots of fun prizes up for grabs!

Speaking of contests, let’s come back to the latest blog writing contest on World of Moms – the This Little Light of Mine contest. With the festival of lights coming up, it’s a great time to think about the one thing that lights up your life and shows you the path forward, no matter what the circumstances may be. In short, think about what inspires you to be the best version of yourself; and blog about it!

All you need to do is write up a lovely blog post and then submit it on the contest page on World of Moms. Of course, you need to be registered with WOM for this, but if you’re not, don’t worry! Just become a member and choose ‘Blogging’ under your hobbies, which will enable you to publish your post on the WOM platform. You can publish a snippet of this post on your blog, with a Read More link back to the original post on WOM. Be sure to include this line below the snippet on your own blog: “This post is an entry for the contest ‘The Little Light of my Life’ on World Of Moms for a jackpot prize”.

Coming to the fun part – prizes!! Here’s a look at the goodies up for grabs:

1st Prize: Clinique Hamper worth Rs. 15,000

2nd Prize: 5 Four Fountain Spa vouchers worth Rs. 2500 each

The first 150 entries get FirstCry vouchers worth Rs. 200 each and there are also surprise giveaways in store! Additionally, the top 3 winners will be featured on the leader-board at WOM. Now, even though it is the world of Moms, you don’t need to be a Mom to participate in this contest; it’s open to all women in India. As for my entry, I’ve talked about my little light, that shows me the path forward even in the darkest of times. To know more about what it is, check out my submission here. So get ready to participate; the contest is open only till the 10th of November, so hurry and get blogging!!
