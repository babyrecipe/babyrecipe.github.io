Ecoware – Eco Friendly Tableware

Ecoware is India’s first and only manufacturer of 100% bio degradable and compostable tableware. These products are completely hygienic and affordably disposable.

Ecoware can be used in birthday parties, travel, vacations, kids excursions or in any event that calls for disposables.

How is Ecoware manufactured?

Ecoware products are manufactured by high temperature moulding of biomass (plant extract or residue) into the desired shape and size using precisely designed moulds. The heat induced pressing gives structural strength to the product and the inter-fiber bonding gives it water and oil resistance.

Advantages of using Ecoware

  • Ecoware is 100 % biodegradable, if you throw an ecoware product on ground,it will degrade into natural compounds and disappear into the soil in 30-60 days.
  • Ecoware is Nontoxic, harmless and healthy, as it uses only plant biomass without any additives, binders, fillers.
  • No plastic or wax lining. The plastic disposables or Styrofoam have a wax lining which have known to cause cancer.
  • No pesticide or chemical residue
  • Does not impart colour, odour or taste to food.
  • Ecoware can be used to serve both hot and cold food items.
  • They can be used in a microwave oven and refrigerator.
  • The products have a shelf life of 2 years.

Certification

The products have been accredited by the USDA Biobased Product Label for 100% Bio degradable Table ware.
