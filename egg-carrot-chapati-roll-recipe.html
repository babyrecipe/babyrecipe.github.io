Egg and Carrot Chapati Roll Recipe

January 30, 2016 Leave a Comment

Plain chapathis can often come across as boring and many kids turn up their noses at the mere mention of them! Moms often come up with innovative ways to present chapathis, and we’ve got one today that’s filling, nutritious and super yummy!

Egg and Carrot Chapathi Roll Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium sized carrots
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 green chilli
  • 1 tsp roasted gram
  • 1/2 tsp urad dal
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 chapati

Method:

1. Heat oil in pan. Add the mustard seeds and urad dal and wait for them to crackle.

2. Add the onions and sauté until they become transparent.

3. Add the ginger garlic paste and fry till the raw smell disappears. Add the tomato and sauté till the oil leaves the sides of the pan.

4. Grate the carrots and add to the onion tomato mixture. Continue to fry till the carrots are completely cooked. Add salt to taste.

5. Beat the eggs lightly and add it to the pan. Stir till the eggs are cooked and mixed well with the vegetables.

6. Now place this filling inside a chapati and roll it tightly. Wrap aluminium foil on one end to hold the filling in. Serve with tomato sauce.

Your yummy egg and carrot chapathi roll is ready! This is a great lunch box option and also makes for a quick lunch or dinner. A great option for Moms who don’t want to spend too much time to cook, but don’t want to order in either!
