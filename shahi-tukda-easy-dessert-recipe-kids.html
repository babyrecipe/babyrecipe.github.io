How to make Shahi Tukda for Toddlers?

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of Brown bread
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp custard powder
  • Ghee for frying


Method:

1. Cut the bread slices into four.

2. Heat ghee in a pan and fry the slices till golden Brown.


3. Take milk in a pan and boil it.


4. Once the milk starts boiling, Â reduce the flame and reduce the milk to half.

5. Add sugar to the reduced milk.

6. Mix custard powder in mildly cold milk.

7. Add this paste to the boiling milk.


8. Once the milk gets thick turn off the gas.

9. Add bread pieces to milk and let it soak the milk.


10. Once itâs ready take it in a plate and serve.
11. Garnish with almond silvers.
