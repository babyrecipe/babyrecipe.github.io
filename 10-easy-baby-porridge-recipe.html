Easy Baby Porridge Recipe

1. Rice Cereal Porridge

Age – Can be given from 6 months

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 2 tablespoon Homemade Rice Cereal Powder

Method

  1. In a pan, bring the water to boil.
  2. Add the Homemade Rice Cereal Powder and stir continuously without lumps.
  3. Stir till it becomes thick and creamy.
  4. Breastmilk or formula can be added to make it runny.
  5. Any fruit can be added for sweetness.

2. Wheat Dalia Porridge

Age – Can be given after 7 months.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon Broken Wheat/ Dalia
  • Roasted Almond Powder – ½ teaspoon

Method

  1. Pressure cook Dalia with 1 cup of water for 3 whistles.
  2. After it cools blend it in a mixer.
  3. Add the almond powder and serve.
  4. Banana/apple puree can be added for sweetness.

Alternatively the dalia can be dry roasted till it emits a nutty fragrance and then powdered. It can be cooked in water for 10 minutes, after cooking the almond powder can be added.

Notes – You can also use our Instant Wheat Dalia Elaichi powder for the same recipe instead of pressure cooking the dalia

3. Ragi Porridge

Age – Can be given from 6 months.

Ingredients

  • A tablespoon of Homemade Ragi powder.
  • ½ cup of water (can be adjusted according to consistency needed.

Method

  1. Mix the ragi powder and water without any lumps.
  2. Heat them in medium flame for 5 to 10 minutes till the ragi is well cooker.

4. Health Mix/ Sathu Maavu Porridge

Age – Can be given from 7 months.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoon Home made Health Mix Powder
  • ½ cup water.

Method

  1. Mix the health mix powder with water without lumps
  2. Place them in medium heat till the porridge is cooked.

7. Apple Rice Cereal Porridge

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoon of Homemade Rice Cereal Powder
  • ½ Apple
  • ½ cup of Water.

Method

  1. Wash and peel the apple
  2. Cut into small pieces
  3. Bring the water to boil.
  4. Add the rice powder and stir it continuously to prevent lump formation.
  5. Add the cut apple pieces.
  6. Stir it till it becomes thick, and both the apple and rice should have cooked well.
  7. Remove from flame, blend it in a mixer for smooth consistency.

8. Sabudana Porridge

Age – Can be given from 7 months

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon Sabudana pearls
  • 1 pinch elachi powder
  • 1 pinch almond powder.

Method

  1. Wash the sabudana in running water few times till the water is clear.
  2. Depending on the type of the sabudana, soak for 15 to 20 minutes (if small) and overnight (if they are large)
  3. In boiling water add the sabudana till it turns translucent.
  4. Switch off the gas.
  5. Garnish with elachi and almond powder.

9. Sooji Porridge

Age – Can be given after 6 months and after starting solids

Ingredients

  • Sooji – 1 cup
  • Ghee – 2 teaspoon
  • Elachi powder – 1 pinch
  • Water – 3 cup

Method

  1. Dry roast sooji in a tawa. It can be either powdered or used as whole.
  2. In a pan, heat the 3 cups of water and allow it to boil.
  3. Add the roasted sooji slowly to the boiling water and mix without lumps.
  4. After the sooji is 1/3 cooked, mix it continuously to prevent lump formation.
  5. When the sooji is 3/4 th cooked, add ghee.
  6. Mix well and switch off the flame after the sooji is well cooked.
  7. A pinch of elachi powder can be added.
  8. Breast milk or formula can be added to make it more runny.

Handy Tips

1. Thinning the porridge can be done with either breast milk or formula.

2. Natural sweeteners like apple or banana can be added.

3. Never add formula while cooking the porridge, add it after the porridge is cooked.

4. Fruits like banana, apple, pear, papaya can be added to the porridge to increase the nutritive value and also enhance the taste.

5. Dry Fruits Powder can also be added to increase the nutritive value.
