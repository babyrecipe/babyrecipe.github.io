How to make Whole Wheat Banana Cake Recipe?

Ingredients:

  • 2 bananas
  • 1.5 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup mixed dry fruits chopped( almonds,walnuts cashew nuts raisins)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • Cinnamon powder a pinch


Method:
1. In a bowl mash the bananas so that there are no lumps.


2. Add in oil sugar and vanilla essence to it so that oil gets mixed properly.


3. In a separate bowl sieve flour baking powder baking soda and cinnamon powder all together.This sieving helps in even mixing.



4. Now add this dry mix gradually to the banana mixture.

5. Add all the fry fruits.


6.You can add a little milk if thr batter is too thick to get a dropping consistency.
7.Grease a cake tin with oil and dust it with flour.


8.Bake for 45 minutes in preheated oven at 220 deg Celsius.


9.Do the toothpick test
10. Your cake is ready.

Serve with milk or hot chocolate.
