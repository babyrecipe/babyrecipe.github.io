Watermelon Lemonade Recipe for Kids

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Watermelon Cubes
  • 3 tbsps honey
  • 5 tbsps freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3 cups of ice cold water.

Method

  1. Blend the Watermelon Cubes and keep them aside.
  2. Add honey, lemon juice salt to cold water.
  3. Mix it well.
  4. Add the blended watermelon juice to the lemonade and mix well.
