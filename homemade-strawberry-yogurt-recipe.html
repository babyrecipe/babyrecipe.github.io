Homemade Strawberry Yogurt Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup hung curd
  • 4-5 strawberries roughly chopped
  • 2 tsp castor sugar

Method

1. Blend all the ingredients together.
2. Pour in a glass.
3. Garnish with s strawberry half or s sprig of mint.
