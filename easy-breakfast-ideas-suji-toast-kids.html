How to make Suji Toast (Easy Breakfast Ideas) for kids?

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup roasted suji
  • 1 cup malai/milk cream fresh
  • 1 carrot grated
  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 1/2 capsicum finely chopped
  • Salt
  • Black pepper powder
  • Ghee to grease the tawa
  • Bread slices


Method:

1. Mix all the ingredients except bread and ghee.

2. Apply a little mixture on a bread slice.


3. Heat a tawa and grease it with little ghee.
4. Put the slice on the tawa with the mixture side down.

5. Cook from both sides till Brown.


Your delicious breakfast or a filling yummy snack is ready.
