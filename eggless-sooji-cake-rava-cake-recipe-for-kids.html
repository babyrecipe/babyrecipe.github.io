Eggless Sooji Cake/Rava Cake Recipe for Kids

February 12, 2015 25 Comments

A simple and a healthy cake to introduce your kiddo to the world of cakes and cookies. This is a Kids friendly Eggless Sooji Cake/Rava Cake/Semolina Cake without maida and ghee replacing butter.

It can be done with the simple home ingredients which is easily available in your kitchen pantry.

How to make Sooji Cake / Rava Cake for Kids?

Ingredients

  • 1 cup suji
  • 1 cup curd
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 4 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Vanilla essence few drops
  • A handful of dry fruits chopped(raisins,cashew nuts,almonds,walnuts)
  • Almond silvers for garnish

Method

1. In a bowl mix suji and powdered sugar together well.
2.Add curd and then milk, mix properly.
3. Add Ghee and mix well.


4. Add baking powder and vanilla essence.


5. Mix all the ingredients together well.
6. Add dryfruits and mix well.


7. Pour the batter in a greased tin.

8. Garnish with almond silvers.

9. Preheat the oven at 180 degree.

10.Bake in the oven at 180deg.

11. Bake till the cake slightly browns in the corners.

See notes for modification of the recipe for babies less than 1 year.

NOTES
* Adjust the sweetness according to your taste.
