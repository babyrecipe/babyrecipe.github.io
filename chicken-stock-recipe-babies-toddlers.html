Chicken Stock Recipe for Babies and Toddlers

January 1, 2015 4 Comments

This chicken stock recipe is designed for babies and toddlers and it serves as a versatile base for many other soups.

Like vegetable stock chicken stock can be used for thinning out purees, can be added to chicken soup and increase the nutritional value of simple foods.

The chicken stock can be frozen and used when needed.

Chicken Stock Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250 gms chicken
  • 1/2 cup carrot chopped
  • 1 onion chopped
  • Bunch of coriander stalks roughly chopped,
  • 4-5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 12 cups of water

Method

  1. Add the chicken into a large stock pot along with the water and add carrot, onion, coriander.
  2. Simmer the flame and bring the stock for the few boils.
  3. Stir occasionally and add salt, minced garlic and bay leaf.
  4. Cover and cook in low flame for an one hour.
  5. Remove the chicken and vegetables
  6. Strain the mixture.
  7. Skim off the fat from surface.
  8. Allow to cool and pour the chicken stock into the jars.
