Ingredients

All you will need is-
  • Blanched and pureed spinach- 4 tbsp
  • Grated garlic- 1 clove (you can skip if your toddler doesnâ€™t like the taste)
  • Wheat flour- 1 tbsp
  • Milk- one and a half cup
  • Black pepper powder- a pinch
  • Butter- 1 tbsp
  • Fresh cream- 2 tbsp
  • Salt to taste
Method

The simple recipe-
1. Take butter in a non-stick pan and heat it.
2. Then add garlic and sautÃ© a little.
3. Add wheat flour and sautÃ© well for 2 minutes. Add milk and keep stirring so as to avoid any lumps.
4. Let the milk come to a boil and add black pepper after lowering the heat.
5. Stir in the spinach puree and let the mixture cook for 2-3 minutes. Keep stirring.
6. Add salt, mix well and remove the pan from heat.
7. Take out the soup in a bowl and add fresh cream.
8. Serve it warm to your little one.
