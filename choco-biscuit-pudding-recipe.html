No Bake Choco Biscuit Pudding Recipe

January 11, 2017 Leave a Comment

Is there a child out there who doesn’t love chocolate? I don’t think so! While we are completely on board with the choco-love, chocolate isn’t exactly good for their teeth and excess chocolate can also result in a tummy ache. So rather than have a full bar of chocolate in one go, it’s better to opt for a chocolate dish that is all chocolate-y, but doesn’t have too much chocolate either. And we’ve got just the recipe – a quick Choco Biscuit Pudding!

Choco Biscuit Pudding Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Biscuit – 1 packet of any biscuit you like
  • Milkmaid / Mithai Mate – ½ tin
  • Cocoa Powder – 4 table spoon
  • Milk – 1/4th bowl or 2 table spoon

Method:

1. Mix milkmaid with coco powder and warm milk to make a thick consistency

2. Add crushed or broken biscuits and mix well.

3. Chill the pudding in a flat bowl for 12 hours. If you like, you can serve with few gems for color.

You can add any biscuits of your choice – go with whole wheat versions to keep it healthier. If your little one has trouble chewing big pieces, break it down further. Make a bowl and serve the whole family – a fun weekend treat!
