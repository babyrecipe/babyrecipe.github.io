Pongal Recipe for Babies

January 15, 2015 7 Comments

My son loves pongal and every other day I used to give to him this pongal recipe that is suitable for Babies.

Pongal is an important festival in my place. Oh How we used to love those days, draw rangolis in front of the house the night before and visit the neighbor’s house in the middle of the night to see their Rangoli. In the mornings, lounge around the house eating sugarcane the whole day while the elders do the preparations for the pongal.

Those days are gone !!!

This pongal recipe can be given from 6 months.

Pongal Recipe for Babies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • ½ cup moong dal
  • 1 teaspoon ghee
  • 1 teaspoon Jeera
  • ¼ inch ginger grated
  • Curry leaves 1 sprig

Method.

  1. Soak rice and moong dal for half an hour.
  2. In a cooker, heat the ghee.
  3. add the jeera and let them splutter
  4. Add the grated ginger and curry leaves and sauté for a minute.
  5. Add the rice and dal
  6. Add 5 cups of water
  7. Allow 4 to 5 whistles on medium flame.
  8. After it cools, remove the curry leaves, mash and serve.
