Beetroot Potato Puree Recipe for Babies

Ingredients

  • Half Potato
  • Half Beetroot

Method
1. Cut a medium sized beetroot and potato into a half.



2. Peel the skin and wash it under running water.



3. Chop them into small cubes.

4. Place them in a katori and inside a pressure cooker filled with water.



5. Allow 2 to 3 whistles.

6. Switch off the flame and remove the katori.

7. Since the beetroot was not cooked soft, I ran the beet and potato in a blender. Some types of beetroot cook nicely others donâ€™t, if it is cooked to a soft consistency, you can mash them with a thick spoon or masher.


8. If it is thick in consistency, add breastmilk or formula feed to make it runny. A pinch of jeera powder can also be added for improving the flavor.

9. Beetroot Potato Puree is ready!!!

This puree combines the goodness of potato and beetroot and the high fiber content of beetroot can counter the gaseous nature of potato
Author: Hemapriya
Recipe type: Puree
Cuisine: Indian
Serves: 1
INGREDIENTS
  • Half Potato
  • Half Beetroot
METHOD
  1. Wash and peel the skin of Potato and beetroot.
  2. Chop them ti fine pieces
  3. Place them in a Katori in a cooker filled with water.
  4. Allow 3 to 4 whistles
  5. Check whether beetroot is cooked well.
  6. You can either mash with a masher or blend it in a mixer
  7. A pinch of jeera powder can be added to enhance the taste
NOTES
If the puree is very thick formula or breastmilk can be added for thinning it

Alternatively for older babies, the blended puree can be sauteed in ghee with a pinch of jeera powder and can be fed.
