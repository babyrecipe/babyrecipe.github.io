How to make Barley Water for Babies ?

January 13, 2015 22 Comments

Barley is one of the perfect first food for babies. Learn how to make barley water for babies below.

Age can be given from 6 months.Hulled barley(not easily available) /Pearl barley (easily available) is ideal for babies

Benefits of Barley

  • Barley can be easily digested.
  • It is nonallergenic and makes perfect first food
  • Barley is rich in fiber, vitamins and minerals

If there is history of celiac disease or gluten allergy, please avoid it in the initial months of weaning.

How to make Barley Water for Babies?

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp barley
  • 2 cups of water

Method

  1. Pressure cook the barley with 2 cups of water
  2. Wash, cool and blend it in a blender.
  3. Strain and allow to cool completely.
  4. Or can be cooled and given as whole porridge
  5. Serve lukewarm.
