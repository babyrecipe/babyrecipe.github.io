INGREDIENTS

  • Jeera - Half teaspoon
  • Curry leaves - a sprig
  • Salt to taste
METHOD
  1. Dry roast all the ingredients.
  2. Let them cool.

  4. Add salt for babies older than 1 year
  5. Chutney powder is ready !!!
  6. Mix with little Ghee and serve with Steamed Dosa, Idli, it can be even mixed with hot rice and ghee.
