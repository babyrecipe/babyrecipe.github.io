This pancake recipe for kids is an Indianized version of the normal pancakes without baking powder and eggs. Toddlers are always fussy eaters and this pancake recipe is sweet enough to lure them to eat ;-). It makes a great finger foodÃ‚ for toddlers.

Wheat Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup wheat flour(atta)
  2. Jaggery syrup (use 100% organic jaggery)
  3. Water
  4. 1 tsp fennel seeds
  5. Ghee

Method

1. Prepare Jaggery syrup.

2. Mix together flour and jaggery.

3. Add fennel seeds to the mixture.

4. Add sufficient water to get the proper consistency. 5. It can also keep it overnight if you want to make the breakfast.

6. Heat the tawa, sprinkle a little ghee.

7. Spread a ladle of batter to get the pancake shape. 8. Cook both sides well.

Wheat Pancake is ready

Tastes great when served with kheer.
