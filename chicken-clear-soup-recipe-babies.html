Chicken Clear Soup Recipe for Babies

December 11, 2014 9 Comments

This is yet another soup recipe for our Winter Series. I am a lazy bone and I always simplify difficult recipes to something that can be done in a jiffy. Try this Easy to cook One Pot Chicken Clear Soup Recipe for your baby. Just add all the ingredients to the cooker and after 3 to 4 whistles it is done.

Chicken for babies

  • It can be given from 7th month
  • It is the richest source of protein and Iron.
  • The thigh and leg flesh are ideal for babies as they are rich in Iron.
  • Organic chicken is ideal

How to make this Chicken Clear soup Recipe?

Chicken clear Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 thigh pieces of chicken.
  • 1 spoon butter.
  • ½ teaspoon jeera.
  • ½ teaspoon coriander seeds.
  • ¼ inch ginger crushed.
  • 2 cloves of garlic crushed.
  • 1 onion chopped.
  • 1 tomato chopped.
  • A pinch of turmeric.

Method

  1. Heat ghee in a cooker.
  2. Add the jeera let them splutter.
  3. Add the coriander seeds
  4. Add the crushed ginger and garlic sauté for a
  5. minute.
  6. Add the onions sauté till transparent.
  7. Add the tomato and fry them till mushy.
  8. Add the turmeric powder.
  9. Add the chicken and sauté for few minutes till
  10. water leaves the chicken.
  11. Add 2 cups of water.
  12. Leave 2 to 3 whistles.
  13. After it cools garnish it with coriander leaves.
  14. Till the baby crosses the eight month sieve off the clear soup and serve
  15. After 8 month can blend everything in blender and can be fed to the baby.
