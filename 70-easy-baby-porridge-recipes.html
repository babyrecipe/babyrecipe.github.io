July 15, 2016 6 Comments

Weaning is a big deal for every Mom and her baby, and some families even have a little celebration for it! The first choice of weaning foods for most Moms is a fruit or vegetable puree, soon followed by slightly more textured foods like porridges. Porridges are a great option for little babies, since it’s easy to prepare and you can pack a good deal of nutrition in a single dish. So to plan your baby’s menu over the coming weeks, we have here a whopping list of over 70 baby porridge recipes, so your baby will never get bored!

Handy Tips for making Baby Porridges

1. Thinning the porridge can be done with either breast milk or formula.

2. Natural sweeteners like apple or banana can be added.

3. Never add formula while cooking the porridge, add it after the porridge is cooked.

4. Fruits like banana, apple, pear, papaya can be added to the porridge to increase the nutritive value and also enhance the taste.

5. Dry Fruits Powder can also be added to increase the nutritive value.
