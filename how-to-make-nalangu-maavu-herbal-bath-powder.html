How to make Nalangu Maavu Herbal Bath Powder?

You can find all the ingredients I have given below in any Ayurvedic herb shops in your city or so-called naatu marundhu kadai.

  • Turmeric/Curcuma longa
  • White turmeric
  • Rose Petals
  • Champak
  • Muthakach khus
  • Indian sarsaparilla
  • Cinnamon
  • Marikolunthu/sagewort
  • Marjoram
  • Flagroot/sweet cane
  • Black stone flower
  • Chinnoti Red Bay Leaf

  1. Sun-dry all the ingredients well for 2 or 3 hours till they became completely moisture free and crisp.
  2. Take the ingredients to the nearby grinding mill and powder them. (But make sure it is grounded in a grinder only for flour since spiciness can irritate the skin)
  3. To retain freshness keep the powder in airtight containers once it is powdered.

Who can use Nalangu Maavu Bath Powder?

Everyone in the family irrespective of gender and kids above 3 years can use Nalangu Maavu Herbal Bath Powder. For boys we don’t add turmeric as it helps shed the body hair, replace the regular turmeric in the ingredients list with green gram dhal for boy babies.

How to use Nalangu Maavu Herbal Bath Powder?

Before using this powder please do a Patch Test to make sure the ingredients are not causing any allergy to your skin. Allergy to the product is rare but better be safe than sorry. It takes nearly 15 minutes to dry completely after which I suggest scrubbing it gently with a little water to exfoliate the skin and then, finally rinsing it off with adequate water.

To use as a daily bath powder:

  • Take the required amount of Nalangu Maavu Herbal Bath Powder and mix some water in it to make a paste. Apply it all over the body and leave for 5 minutes and then rinse it off with water. Or you can even use it as a dry scrub by simply scrubbing the skin with Nalangu Maavu Herbal Bath Powder and then washing it off with water.

To use it as a face wash:

  • Make a paste of Nalangu Maavu Herbal Bath Powder with water.
  • People with dry skin types can use curd or milk fat for better results.
  • For normal skin water or curd or honey can be added.

Things to remember while making Nalangu Maavu Herbal Bath Powder:

  • If you are allergic to any of the ingredients you can avoid it while preparing Nalangu Maavu Herbal Bath Powder.
  • Try to make it during summer, as it will ensure thorough drying of the ingredients. If you powder it using a mixer at home make sure the jar is washed well and dried completely as it shouldn’t have even a tinge of spiciness and moisture. Spiciness can irritate the skin and moisture can spoil the powder.
  • I usually prepare Nalangu Maavu Herbal Bath Powder in large quantity and store it in airtight containers and use it for a year.

For Daily Use:

  • You can make a huge batch of this herbal powder and store it for up to a year. You can transfer a small quantity of it to a container /bottle for daily use and keep the rest airtight in another container.
