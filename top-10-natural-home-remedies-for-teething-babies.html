Many new mothers dread teething. They’ve heard so much about teething woes – tender swollen gums, lots of drool – that can change the happiest kid on the planet into a cranky, irritable and grumpy little monster! The discomfort is because of baby teeth cutting the gums as they erupt. Parenting has its unpleasant parts, and teething probably tops the list! The child may refuse to feed, cries and stays irritable. Or she may keep asking for more feeds, in an effort to find some comfort.

Is there a way out of this? Of course there is! And not just one, here are 10 home remedies for teething blues, that are completely natural and guaranteed to soothe your little one and relax you as well!

3. MASSAGE: Gently massage the child’s gums with your fingers. This provides relief to the sore gums and all you need is a clean finger.

6. CLOVE OIL: Clove oil also has numbing effect which helps to dull the pain.

7. VANILLA EXTRACT: Vanilla isn’t just yummy, it also has a calming and soothing effect on pain.

8. GINGER: A small piece of ginger works as an anti-inflammatory when rubbed on the gums.

9. COOL SPOON: This is a tip from the American Dental Association – a clean steel spoon can be chilled and given to the baby.

10. ALMOND EXTRACT: Mix a little almond extract with water and rub on the gums for pain relief.
