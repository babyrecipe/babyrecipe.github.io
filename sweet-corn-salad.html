Sweet Corn Salad – Refreshing Summer Snack

April 10, 2017 Leave a Comment

We launch our Mums in the Kitchen Series today with a fresh Sweet Corn Salad Recipe. Watch this space all week for a new recipe every day!

Today, we have a lovely, refreshing salad that’s perfect for summer. Vibrantly colored, this Sweet Corn Salad makes a fun snack for kids. This summer salad is succulent, sweet, juicy and crunchy all at the same time. Pair the sweet corn with veggies, toss with lime juice, season and you are done!

Sweet Corn Salad – Refreshing Summer Snack

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cup sweet corn kernels
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, finely chopped
  • Handfull of coriander leaves , finely chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup Pomegranate arils
  • 2 teaspoon butter
  • Chaat masala to taste
  • Salt to taste

Method:

1. Rinse the sweet corn kernels in running water and add them to boiling water.

2. Cook the kernels for 2-3 minutes. Drain and keep aside. Alternatively you can cook them in microwave.

3. In a pan, melt butter on a low flame. Add the cooked and drained sweet corn kernels and saute for 1-2 minutes. Put off the flame and transfer the kernels in a mixing bowl.

4. Add chopped onions, tomatoes , coriander leaves and pomegranate seeds to the sweet corn. Mix well.

5. Season the salad with lime juice, salt and chaat masala.

This sweet corn salad is nutritious and a great alternative to the traditional chick pea salad. This dish works great as a snack or as a colorful side dish to brighten up your meal. You don’t need to serve ice cream every time the kids ask for a snack – serve this to them instead!
