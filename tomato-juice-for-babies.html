Tomato Juice for Babies

July 12, 2014 2 Comments

It is yet another very easy recipe which takes about just 10 minutes of your time.

There are mixed opinions on whether tomatoes can be given to babies. Now AAP has recently published that early (after 6 months) introduction of a variety of foods can actually minimize the risk of future allergies in children. (Reference quoted below)

Method

Sterilize all the vessels used for making baby food.

1. A medium sized tomato yield about 2 tablespoons of juice.Â



2. Wash and chop the tomatoes into medium sized pieces.



3. In a bowl, take a half cup of water and allow it to boil.



4. When the water starts boiling switch off the gas and adds the chopped tomatoes to it.



5. Leave it aside for 15 minutes.


6. The skin would have peeled after soaking in hot water, gently peel the skin of all the pieces.



7. Mash the contents with a masher or a thick spoon.



8. Sieve the contents.



9. Tomato juice is ready !!!
