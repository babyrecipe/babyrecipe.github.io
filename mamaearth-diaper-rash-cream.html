What is a diaper rash?

A diaper rash is a rash seen on the skin of a baby’s buttocks, usually before their first birthday. A diaper rash can appear as a reddish rash, showing the skin’s irritation. Diaper rashes have many causes – it could be when the acidity of the stool changes, especially when babies start solids. It could worsen in babies who begin to sit on their buttocks. It can also be due to an allergy to the diaper or to the detergent used to wash cloth diapers. Whatever the cause may be, it can make your baby miserable!!
