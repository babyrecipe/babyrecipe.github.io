How to make Apple Oats Porridge for Babies

IngredientsÂ

  • Half apple
  • 100 gm of 100% Organic Oats

Method

1.Wash and Peel half an apple.

2. Chop them into medium sized pieces.

3. In a pan, boil water and add oats to it.

4. Add the chopped apple to the oats.

5. Cook the porridge till the oats are nicely cooked and the apple turns mushy.

6. Blend it in a mixer.

7. Yummy Apple Oats Porridge is ready.

For Toddlers, 50 ml of milk can be added in last and allowed to boil for a minute and switch off the gas.

A yummy porridge with oats and apple without any sweetening agent for babies
Author: Hemapriya
Recipe type: Porridge
Cuisine: Indian
Serves: 2
INGREDIENTS
  • Half apple
  • 100 gm Oats (whole or powdered)
  • 1 cup of water
METHOD
  1. Wash and peel the apple.
  2. You can either chop the apple into small pieces or grate the apple.
  3. Boil the water and add the oats to the boiling water.
  4. After the oats have mixed well add the apple and cover with a lid.
  5. Switch off the gas after the apple and oats are well cooked.
  6. Blend it in a mixie. If you use powdered oats then blending is not necessary
NOTES
For Toddlers, you can add milk after the oats and apple is cooked well.
