Watermelon Smoothie Recipe for Babies and Toddlers

May 13, 2015 9 Comments

This Watermelon Smoothie recipe is a refreshing summer drink for Kids with curd and milk with the goodness of watermelon.

This watermelon smoothie can be given to babies after 8 months without milk.

For toddlers you can use milk with curd.

Watermelon Smoothie Recipe for Babies and Toddlers

Ingredients

  • 3 cups 1/2-inch chunks of seedless watermelon (the smaller the chunks, the easier it is to blend this watermelon smoothie)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 3 Tbsp Honey

Method

1. Cut the watermelon into small pieces.

2. If you want a cool smoothie, you can refrigerate the watermelon 2 hours prior.

3. Blend everything together and serve chill.

Instead of Honey you can also use maple syrup or dates syrup.
