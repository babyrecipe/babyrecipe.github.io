Maize Porridge Recipe for Babies

December 5, 2014 4 Comments

The Maize Porridge can be given to babies after 9 months. It is an ideal travel food for babies.

This flour is also called as Makai Ki Atta or corn flour.

How to make Maize Porridge for babies at Home?

Maize Porridge

Ingredients :

Roasted Maize Powder 50 g.
Roasted Moong dal Powder 20 g.
Roasted Til Powder (gingelly or seasame seed) 10 g.

Method of preparation:

1. Mix all the ingredients thoroughly.
2. Store in dry and air tight container.

Nutritive value per 100 gms:

Calories 377
Protein 12 .28 g.
Iron 2 .87 mg.
Carotene 61 μg.
