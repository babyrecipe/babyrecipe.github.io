How to prepare the Instant Porridge ?

  1. Take about 4 Table spoons (according to your requirement, this is a sample measurement) of mix.
  2. Add 100 ml of boiled hot water to get a thick consistency.
  3. Add a tea spoon of oil or ghee to increase the energy content.
  4. Feed the infant with the spoon.
  5. The instant porridge can also be fed to the child after reconstituting with dal, water, gravy, milk etc.
  6. Mashed fruit (Banana, Chiku, Papaya, Mango, etc.could also be added to the food).

The powders have a shelf life of 6 months if preserved in an airtight container.
