How & When to Add Spices in Baby Food?

The spices can be added to Purees, Porridges, Khichdi, Sheera, Kheer and almost all baby foods. Depending on the spice they can be added in different stages of baby food.

All the spices are to be added only after starting solids and never give raw spices before starting solids as it may do more harm than good. Please follow the 3 day rule before introducing any new spice.
